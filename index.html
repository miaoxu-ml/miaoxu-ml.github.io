Short Biography

    I am now a lecturer at the School of ITEE, University of Queensland. I was a postdoc researcher at RIKEN AIP, supervised by Prof. Masashi Sugiyama. I received my B.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees in computer science from Nanjing University in 2009 and 2017, respectively, under the supervision of Prof. Zhi-Hua Zhou. I was a member of the LAMDA Group, and was a visiting scholar at Michigan State University, supervised by Prof. Rong Jin.

Research

    My research interests include machine learning and its real-world application. Now I am working on weakly supervised learning.
