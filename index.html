Brief Biography

    Miao Xu is a postdoc researcher at RIKEN AIP, supervised by Prof. Masashi Sugiyama. She pursued her Ph.D. from the Computer Science Department at Nanjing University, supervised by Prof. Zhi-Hua Zhou and was a visiting scholar at Michigan State University, supervised by Prof. Rong Jin. Her research focuses on machine learning and its real-world applications. She has published papers on conferences and journals including ICML, NeurIPS, AAAI, IJCAI, KDD, and TKDE. Her long-term goal is to develop intelligent systems, which can learn from a massive volume of weakly supervised data. She has served as program committees to ICML, NeurIPS, AISTATS, AAAI, IJCAI, and ACML. She received the NJU Presidential Fellowship (2014), IBM Ph.D. Fellowship (2015), and CCF Outstanding Doctoral Dissertation Award Nomination (2018).
